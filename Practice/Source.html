Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Srinivas Boga

MBBS (GOLD MEDALIST), MS (ORTHO-OSM), DNB (ORTHO), FIJR CONSULTANT
Trauma, Arthroscopy, and Joint Replacement Surgeon

Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Srinivas Boga is an award-winning Orthopedic Surgeon, holding prestigious qualifications in orthopedics including MBBS (Gold Medalist), MS (Ortho-OSM), DNB (Ortho), and FIJR Consultant. Specializing in trauma, arthroscopy, and joint replacement, he offers comprehensive care for orthopedic injuries, conditions, and diseases.

His approach emphasizes non-surgical treatments, such as physical therapy, aimed at promoting recovery without invasive procedures. Dr. Srinivas is also an expert in minimally invasive arthroscopic surgeries, which ensure quicker recovery and less discomfort for patients.

As the founder of his practice, Dr. Srinivas, along with a team of board-certified orthopedic specialists, uses cutting-edge treatments to deliver personalized care plans. Whether treating minor injuries or performing complex joint replacements, his goal is to provide the best possible care.
